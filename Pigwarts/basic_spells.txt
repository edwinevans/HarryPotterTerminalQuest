

Basic Spells

pwd - Show where you are on the magical map ("printus working directorium")
ls - Look around for books and rooms ("list")
cat [book name] - Read a book ("catenate"). For example "cat basic_spells.txt".
cd - Teleport into a different room ("changus directorium")

TIP: You can sometimes wave the "tab" key to quickly complete a spell or name. Use with caution.
